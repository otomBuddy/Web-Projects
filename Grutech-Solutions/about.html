Background Info

Gurutech was founded in 2018 as an idea of three IT students (Benard Otom, Duncan Gwada and Hillary Kima) at Kisii University. The main objective of coming up with the idea was to provide IT services to students around the campus at affordable prices. The services offered at the start were Windows OS installation, Hardware repairs, Password and data recoveries among others.

Who We Are

We are a group of developers and network engineers who are enthusiastic techie guys that specialize in computer programming (Python, Java, C, C++, PHP, Js, CSS and HTML 5) and network engineering (CCNA and HCIA). Besides being the young programmers and network engineers, our team are also skilled in website developments, database (Firebase and mySQL), Software and hardware installation, configuration, monitoring and repairs. Our Vision

To provide quality and affordable IT services to the students around Kisii University and the community at large as we also learn and share ideas.

Our Mision

To enable IT services to have a smooth operational abilities and every student or community member have their IT problems fixed.

Our Values

Integrity. Boldness. Honesty. Fairness. Trustworthiness. Accountability. Learning. Customer Experience.
